STORY of MY LIFE

Early Years (1999-2005)

It all started when I was born. Then the next thing I knew was that childhood would become the bane of my entire existence. But I found comfort knowing there were other ways to go down. Hi I'm Bianca, but if my father had not stepped in during birth, you may call me Daffodil Cattleya Tulip Rose.

In the Name of Love...
(2005-Present)

I fell in love with a lot of guys but most of them weren't worth the trouble in the end. I'm certain that as soon as I find the love of my life, the universe will sing at how I've "finally got it!".

How to Pass IT? (2018-2023)

IT? Well, let me tell you something about myself: I don't know how to code to save my life.
Well not exactly--I'm just really more on the front end development of things as opposed to working on the inner mechanisms of things.
So I guess goodluck to me but I'm a pure html/css/bootstrap/javascript kinda gal UwU.

The Magic of Tarot (2019)

During a time of great emotional and psychological instability, I stumbled upon Tarot reading and it has brought me more peace than anything ever. Right now I'm a full blown pantheist but don't confuse it for witchcraft: It just means that I see God and the Universe as the same beings, and having readingshelp make the unknown future more tangible, giving me more options on how I might want to do things.

Ah yes, Children

Truth be told, whenever I see children running around I want to emit the darkest energy I have and direct it at them without the slightest feeling of guilt. But on the other side of things, I actually would want some of those little things: I want 3 of them to be exact!

Naming of Children

I often see that I might have 2 boys and 1 girl and so just in case I have already prepared names for them: Aziraphale, Nathaniel, and Eclipsa. Don't worry-I'll leave the more normal second names to my husband's judgement so that the little ones can choose if they wanna be weird or not haha.

Bury Me Softly

Life for the most part is good but sometimes I want to be burried without hesitation because that's when the best things come to life: Like free flowers or words of affirmation in your eulogy. All comedy is derived from fear, so who's to say that there can't be beauty in death?
